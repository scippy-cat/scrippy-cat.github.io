Why is Scippy called Scippy?

Scippy is called scippy because a couple of years ago I saw a stray cat lying on top of a bin looking for food. I remember that memory till this very day! When we got Scippy that memory of that stray cat filled my head. Then I rememberd that the cat was lying on the bin and thought who calls their cat binny. So I serched online and found out that some bins were called skips. So I decided to call it Skippy . I got the in- sparation from my friends dog fluffy. Then I thought that skippy was to boring and anyone can spell it so I called my cat Scippy.

When I first met Scippy

When I first met Scippy he was just three monthes old and very cute. At the breeders house I couden't tell wich one I wanted. Before that I was really into a cat called Chestnut but then I saw Scippy and was enticed by him so I was stuck between the two cats so I chose Scippy.
